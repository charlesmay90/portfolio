Musicality

Musicality is an interactive application that allows users to search multiple music related websites at once. Users can type in one of their favorite bands or artists and get a list of Youtube search results, upcoming live concerts through Ticket master, as well as a link to the band wikipedia page. It's a one stop shop for all music lovers.
